🔧 ML Pipeline

The complete workflow from raw SMS messages to spam classification.

01

Load Data

Load the SMS Spam Collection dataset from UCI — 5,572 real-world SMS messages labeled as spam or ham.

pandasUCI Dataset
02

Text Preprocessing

Lowercase, remove URLs/emails/HTML, strip punctuation, tokenize, remove stopwords, and apply Porter Stemming.

NLTKRegExStemming
03

Feature Extraction

Convert cleaned text into TF-IDF feature vectors (max 5,000 features) with unigram + bigram n-grams.

TF-IDFN-gramssklearn
04

Train/Test Split

Stratified 80/20 split preserving the ham/spam ratio in both training and test sets.

Stratified80-20
05

Model Training

Train 6 classifiers (Naive Bayes, Logistic Regression, SVM, Random Forest, Gradient Boosting) with 5-fold CV.

6 Models5-Fold CV
06

Evaluation

Measure with accuracy, precision, recall, F1-score, ROC curves, and confusion matrices.

F1-ScoreROC-AUC
